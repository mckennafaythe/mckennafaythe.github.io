White Space and Clean Design

Clek Inc.

clekinc.com

The Clek mobile site uses white space and clean design by leaving ample space between elements in the header, as well as between the text and buttons in the main body of the page. Clean design is shown again in the simplistic use of color.

P.A.R.C. Repetition

Discover

discover.com

Repetition is seen in Discover's mobile site through their use of color and box shapes. The site is easier to navigate because the repition of orange points you to where you'd like to click.

Hick's Law

YouTube

youtube.com

Hick's Law argues that more choices = more time to decide, and in turn, the possibility to not make a decision at all. Youtube's full site has many options, but they did a great job of narrowing it down to a bar of 4 at the bottom. Concisely displaying the most important links makes this site more user-friendly.
